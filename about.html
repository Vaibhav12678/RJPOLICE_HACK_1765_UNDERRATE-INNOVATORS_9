About Us

The Rajasthan Police is well-prepared to handle any issue pertaining to cybercrime safety and security in this age of advanced information technology and the internet boom. Our dedication lies in limiting and/or resolving any cybercrime involving the improper use of digital equipment, including as computers, laptops, and mobile phones, both with and without internet access, as well as putting cyber security regulations into practice. With the establishment of a cutting-edge control room to oversee and assist with cyber security throughout the state, Rajasthan Police is now offering its assistance to the people of Rajasthan. To get help with internet frauds, cyberbullying, or crimes like identity or data theft, any person can go to the police control center.
